About Us

Zoom classes take place in a group or 1:1. Extra classes can be arranged and all are very welcomed!

Classes will be recorded. These class recordings will be made available upon request.

Any additional information or tutoring will be made available upon request.

All questions are geared towards final examination of the Junior Cycle.

What is comfortable to the student is the main focus of learning. This is student centric and every opportunity is given to make sure that knowledge is attainable and that classes are enjoyable.

Slack is a great way to get "tips of the day", to find out what is going on. This is a place for students to get together and build long lasting friendships.
